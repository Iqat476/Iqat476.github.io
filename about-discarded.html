ABOUT ME:

Who Am I?

Born beneath the sun-kissed skies of Abu Dhabi, UAE, and enriched by the cultural tapestry of my Pakistani heritage from Karachi, I embody a fusion of diverse influences that have shaped my worldview. I embarked on my Cambridge International O-Levels at the tender age of 14, choosing the route of a private candidate.

In the realm of knowledge, I have discovered a haven within the intricate world of programming. Beyond the sleek interfaces visible to users, I find fascination in unraveling the intricate codes and algorithms that breathe life into the technologies we encounter daily.
